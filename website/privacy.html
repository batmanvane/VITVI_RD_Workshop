5. Registration Form

When you register for the workshop via the registration form, we collect the following data:

  • Name, email address, affiliation (organization)
  • Role / position
  • Participant type (PhD Researcher / Senior Participant)
  • Research area / expertise (optional)
  • Presentation preference (optional)
  • Topic suggestions (optional)
  • Dietary requirements (optional)
  • Hotel needs (optional)

The data is used exclusively for the organization and execution of the workshop. Submissions are transmitted via Formspree (Formspree, Inc., USA). Formspree processes the data on our behalf and forwards it to us by email. For more information, see Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent by submitting the form). You may withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future.

6. Email Inquiries

If you contact us by email (e.g. via the inquiry function for non-listed organizations), we process your email address and message content to handle your request.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to inquiries).
