## What this is

This is not a traditional academic conference. There are no polished keynotes, no 20-slide thesis overviews. Instead, each PhD student gets 30 minutes to open up their real work to the room — either a **Problem Pitch** (a concrete roadblock they're stuck on) or a **Breakthrough Pitch** (a finding they want stress-tested and cross-pollinated).

Senior participants from industry and academia join as brain-trust: asking hard questions, sharing experience, and helping unblock research.

## What we need from you

**PhD researchers** — pick one:

- **Problem Pitch:** You have a specific technical roadblock. Present it in 10 minutes (strict 4-slide rule), then get 20 minutes of group troubleshooting. Come with your broken code, failed experiments, and dirty data.
- **Breakthrough Pitch:** You have a finding worth sharing. Present it in 10 minutes (5-slide rule), then get 20 minutes of stress-testing and transfer discussion.

**Senior participants** — join as audience and brain-trust. No presentation required. Your experience and perspective are the contribution.
